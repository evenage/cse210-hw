Video Class

Attributes:

Title: The title of the video (String).
Author: The name of the video's author (String).
Length: The length of the video in seconds (Integer).
Comments: A list to store comments associated with the video (List of Comment).
Methods:

AddComment(Comment comment): Adds a comment to the Comments list.
GetNumberOfComments(): Returns the total number of comments (Integer).
DisplayInfo(): Displays video details (title, author, length, and all comments).

Comment Class

Attributes:
Name: The name of the person who made the comment (String).
Text: The content of the comment (String).
